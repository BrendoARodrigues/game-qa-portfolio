| Status             | Confirmed                             |
| Category           | Collision / Environment               |
| Area               | Phase 3 — End Section                 |
| Reproduction Rate  | Multiple successful reproductions     |

---

## Description

At the end of Phase 3, giant gear-like structures rotate and periodically strike a wall. If the player stands near the gear at the moment it makes contact with the wall, the player character clips inside the gear's geometry.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Progress to the end section of Phase 3.
2. Locate the giant gear-like structures that rotate and hit the wall.
3. Position the player character near the gear, close to the wall it strikes.
4. Wait for the gear to complete its rotation and impact the wall.
5. Observe whether the player clips inside the gear geometry.

---

## Expected Result

The gear's collision should push or block the player character without allowing any overlap between the player and the environmental object. The player should not be able to occupy the same space as solid geometry.

---

## Actual Result

When standing near the gear during its wall-contact moment, the player character clips inside the gear structure. This results in the player being embedded in moving geometry, potentially becoming trapped or displaced from the intended play area.

---

## Impact

The player may get stuck inside a moving environmental object, which could cause them to be pushed through the wall or into an unintended area. In a Phase 3 end section, this collision failure could disrupt a critical gameplay moment or prevent normal progression.

---

## Severity Justification

**Severity: Medium** — Clipping inside a moving environmental structure can trap the player or push them out of bounds, disrupting progression at a significant area of the game.

**Priority: Medium** — Occurs at a specific and repeatable point in Phase 3. Conditions are well-defined and the potential for a player to get stuck or be pushed out of bounds justifies timely investigation.
